Welcome to Sahasra College's MPC program! It's fantastic to see an institution that values the importance of a strong foundation in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry for aspiring students in the field of engineering and technology.

The presence of experienced educators and subject matter experts in your faculty ensures that students receive top-notch guidance and mentoring throughout their academic journey.
