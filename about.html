About us

Sahasra IIT & Medical Academy stands as a beacon of educational excellence, particularly in its MPC and BIPC programs. Founded by the visionary leader, Manepalli Venkateswara Rao, with over 33 years of teaching experience, the institution prioritizes a well-rounded education. Its experienced faculty not only imparts theoretical knowledge but also integrates practical experiences, enriching lessons with real-world examples and case studies. The institution is committed to ensuring students gain the skills and knowledge needed to excel in competitive fields like engineering, technology, medicine, dentistry, and pharmacy.



The academy's hostel facilities prioritize comfort, safety, and convenience, offering a home-like atmosphere within walking distance of the college campus. With spacious 2-bedded rooms accommodating up to 4 members, the hostel fosters lasting bonds and memories. Emphasizing cleanliness, security, and exceptional food facilities, Sahasra ensures a conducive environment for both study and relaxation. Recreational activities every Sunday provide students with a rejuvenating break, fostering a sense of community and fun among peers.



Sahasra's specialized study hour program reflects its commitment to personalized support for students with special needs. Recognizing the diversity in learning styles and challenges, the institution's experienced tutors collaborate with each student to create customized study plans, ensuring every learner has the opportunity to succeed academically.



The academy's commitment to providing top-notch resources extends to well-equipped labs that facilitate hands-on learning and research across various disciplines. Boasting over 800 students, Sahasra College offers a vibrant community of learners with a range of programs, modern facilities, and a dynamic campus life. The distinguished faculty, each with over 30 years of experience, contributes to a rich academic environment.



With 20+ air-conditioned classrooms, Sahasra College ensures a comfortable and productive learning environment. The sprawling 1+1 campus offers an inspiring backdrop for learning, growth, and exploration, making Sahasra College an ideal choice for those seeking academic excellence and personal development.
